Why classics need specialist transport

The wrong recovery truck can damage what took you decades to build.

📐

Tilt-bed loading angle

The bed lowers nearly flat for low-clearance cars. No splitter scrape, no ramp damage, no air-dam crushed.

🪢

Soft axle straps

No chains. No ratchet straps cutting into the wishbones. Soft over-the-tyre straps that don't compress the suspension or stress the bodywork.

🚗

Single-vehicle bed

Your car travels alone. No lorry-load convoy. No transhipment. Loaded once, unloaded once.

🛡️

Goods-in-transit insured

Cover from the moment we load until the moment we unload. Higher-value cover available on request — let us know in advance.

📸

Photo & condition record

Full-circle photos at pickup and drop-off, sent by WhatsApp. Existing marks documented and dated.

🌧️

Covered transport on request

For long-haul or particularly precious cars, we can fit a temporary tarp/cover. Discuss in advance and we'll quote it.
