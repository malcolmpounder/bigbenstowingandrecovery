Why scrap with us not direct?

Honest answer: if your car runs and you can drive it to a scrapyard yourself, you'll sometimes get a touch more. But if it doesn't run, or you'd rather not faff with the trip and the paperwork, we save you the hassle and the breakdown call-out.
