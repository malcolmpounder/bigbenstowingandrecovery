How trade accounts work

Set it up once, then it's frictionless every job after.

01

Drop us a line

Email info@bigbenstowingandrecovery.co.uk with your business name, billing contact and typical job profile. We'll get back to you within a working day.

02

We agree your rate

Per-mile rate based on volume. Standing charges optional for fleet contracts. No call-out fees, ever.

03

You raise a PO

Per-job purchase order, or a master PO with a monthly summary. Whatever fits your accounts process.

04

We move the vehicle

Photo at pickup, photo at delivery, signed POD. Goods-in-transit insured throughout. Direct WhatsApp line for live updates.

05

Monthly invoice, 30-day terms

Clean breakdown by vehicle, reg, route and mileage. BACS payment. Late payments handled in line with our standard terms.
