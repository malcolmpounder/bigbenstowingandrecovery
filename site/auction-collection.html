How a collection works

Five short steps from auction win to delivery.

01

Send us the gate pass

Auction confirmation / gate release / lot number, plus your delivery address and any storage deadline.

02

We confirm a slot

Same-day if it's a local yard and the vehicle's ready. Otherwise, the soonest available scheduled slot.

03

We collect & photograph

Full-circle photo handover by WhatsApp before we move. Existing damage on the auction sheet — confirmed and dated.

04

Goods-in-transit covered

Insured from the moment we load. The vehicle stays on the bed — no transhipment unless specifically agreed.

05

Delivery + signed POD

Photo on arrival, signed proof of delivery, invoice on the agreed account terms.
