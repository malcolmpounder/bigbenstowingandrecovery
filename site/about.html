Why us not them

An honest read on what we do well and where we're not the right fit.

When we're the right call

  • You're broken down in the North East and want a real person fast.
  • You've sold or bought a vehicle and need it moved by someone who'll treat it like their own.
  • You've got a classic, EV, motorbike or low-slung car that shouldn't be flat-towed.
  • You're a bodyshop, dealership or insurer wanting trade rates with PO accounts.
  • You'd rather scrap a car for cash than pay a garage to MOT a write-off.

When we're probably not

  • You want a subscription breakdown contract — RAC and AA do that better than us.
  • You need an HGV-rated heavy recovery — we run a single tilt-bed flatbed, not a wrecker fleet.
  • You want covered/enclosed transport for a £200k+ supercar — we'll quote it but a specialist enclosed-trailer firm may suit better.

Base & equipment

Based on the A1(M) corridor at Great Lumley (DH3 4HU), with quick access to:

  • A1(M) — Newcastle north, Darlington south
  • A19 — Sunderland, Teesside, Tyneside east coast
  • A66 — Penrith / Cumbria for transport jobs
  • M1 / M6 — long-haul UK-wide via the A1(M) ↔ A66 link

The truck is a modern Iveco tilt-bed flatbed — soft loading angle, padded contact points, rated lashing. Equipped with a winch, vehicle dolly, soft straps for bikes, and a 12V boost pack for stuck EVs and dead auxiliaries.
